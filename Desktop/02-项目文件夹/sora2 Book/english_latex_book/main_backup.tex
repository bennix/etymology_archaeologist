\textbf{Teaching and Academic Activities:}

In his teaching capacity, Professor Xu has developed and taught innovative, interdisciplinary courses that bridge technology and practical applications. Notably, he has offered the course "Introduction to Virtual Reality," which emphasizes hands-on, cross-disciplinary learning experiences.

His pedagogical approach involves leading undergraduate students in practical projects that utilize VR technology for cultural preservation and educational purposes. A significant example of this work occurred during Fudan University's 116th anniversary celebration, when Professor Xu guided 10 undergraduate students enrolled in his VR course to create a virtual reality "restoration" of the Fudan University History Museum.

This ambitious project encompassed:
\begin{itemize}
\item 984 historical images from the museum's collection
\item 978 textual labels and descriptions
\item Coverage of approximately 95\% of the museum's exhibition content
\end{itemize}

Professor Xu has also contributed to the design of VR exhibitions for "University Party Building Innovation Practice Bases," combining Fudan's institutional history with the Communist Party's historical narrative through immersive virtual reality experiences.

His work represents a commitment to leveraging emerging technologies for educational innovation, cultural preservation, and the advancement of human-computer interaction in academic and cultural contexts.

\section*{Welcome to the Future of Filmmaking}

We stand at the threshold of a revolutionary moment in creative expression. The emergence of AI-powered video generation, exemplified by OpenAI's Sora2, represents more than a technological advancement—it marks the democratization of cinematic storytelling. For the first time in history, the barrier between imagination and visual realization has been reduced to the power of language itself.

This book is your comprehensive guide to mastering this new creative frontier. Whether you're a filmmaker seeking to expand your toolkit, a content creator exploring new possibilities, or an AI enthusiast curious about creative applications, this manual will transform your understanding of what's possible when artificial intelligence meets human creativity.

\section*{Who This Book Is For}

\textbf{Primary Audience:}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Content Creators and Filmmakers:} Independent filmmakers, video producers, social media creators, and digital artists seeking to integrate AI video generation into their creative workflow
\item \textbf{Creative Professionals:} Marketing professionals, brand storytellers, advertising creatives, and communication specialists who need to produce compelling visual content efficiently
\item \textbf{AI Enthusiasts and Technologists:} Developers, researchers, and technology professionals interested in the practical applications of generative AI in creative industries
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Secondary Audience:}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Educators and Students:} Film school instructors, digital media educators, and students studying the intersection of technology and creative arts
\item \textbf{Business Leaders:} Executives and entrepreneurs exploring AI's potential for content marketing, brand communication, and digital transformation
\item \textbf{Creative Hobbyists:} Individuals passionate about storytelling, visual arts, and emerging technologies who want to explore new forms of creative expression
\end{itemize}

\section*{What You'll Learn}

This book balances technical precision with creative inspiration, providing:

\textbf{Technical Mastery:} Step-by-step guidance for accessing, configuring, and optimizing Sora2 for professional-quality output

\textbf{Creative Methodology:} Systematic approaches to prompt engineering, visual storytelling, and narrative construction using AI tools

\textbf{Professional Applications:} Real-world case studies, commercial applications, and industry best practices for AI-assisted filmmaking

\textbf{Ethical Framework:} Comprehensive coverage of legal considerations, ethical guidelines, and responsible AI usage in creative contexts

\section*{How to Use This Book}

\textbf{For Beginners:} Start with Chapters 1-3 to build foundational understanding, then progress through the practical applications in Chapters 4-6

\textbf{For Experienced Creators:} Focus on Chapters 5-8 for advanced techniques, then reference Chapter 9 for professional and ethical considerations

\textbf{For Technical Professionals:} Chapters 2, 6, and 8 provide detailed technical frameworks and systematic methodologies

\textbf{For Business Applications:} Chapters 7 and 9 offer commercial case studies and professional implementation strategies

\section*{A Note on Rapid Evolution}

AI video generation technology evolves at unprecedented speed. While this book focuses on Sora2 as of January 2025, the principles, methodologies, and creative frameworks presented here are designed to remain relevant as the technology advances. We emphasize adaptable thinking over platform-specific techniques, ensuring your skills grow with the technology.

\section*{Join the Creative Revolution}

You're about to embark on a journey that will fundamentally change how you think about visual storytelling. The fusion of human creativity and artificial intelligence opens possibilities we're only beginning to explore. This book is your roadmap to that future.

Welcome to the age where language becomes light, where words become worlds, and where every creator has the power of a Hollywood studio at their fingertips.
